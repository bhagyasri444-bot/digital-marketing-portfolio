Google Ads Campaign Strategy – Desi Chowrasta

## Business Overview
Desi Chowrasta is an Indian restaurant located in Austin, Texas offering South Indian dishes, biryani, and vegetarian meals.

## Campaign Objective
Increase local restaurant visibility and online food orders.

## Target Audience
Location: Austin, Texas

Age: 22–45

Audience Interests:
Indian food lovers
South Indian cuisine
Vegetarian food
Biryani lovers

## Campaign Structure

Campaign Name:
Desi Chowrasta – Search Campaign

Ad Group 1 – Dosa
Keywords:
dosa near me
best dosa in Austin
South Indian dosa restaurant

Sample Ad:
Headline: Best Dosa in Austin
Description: Authentic South Indian dosa served fresh daily.

Ad Group 2 – Biryani
Keywords:
hyderabadi biryani Austin
best biryani near me
Indian biryani restaurant

Sample Ad:
Headline: Authentic Hyderabadi Biryani
Description: Traditional spices and rich flavors.

Ad Group 3 – Vegetarian Food
Keywords:
vegetarian Indian restaurant
veg Indian food Austin

Sample Ad:
Headline: Fresh Vegetarian Indian Meals
Description: Delicious healthy vegetarian dishes.

## Budget Strategy
Daily Budget: $20

Monthly Budget: $600

## Conversion Tracking

Track the following actions:
Online orders
Menu page visits
Phone calls
Location directions

Tools Used:
Google Ads
Google Analytics

## Optimization Plan
Monitor CTR and conversions
Add negative keywords
Improve ad copy
Adjust bids for high-performing keywords

## Expected KPIs

Impressions: 10,000
Clicks: 300
CTR: 3%
Conversions: 20
